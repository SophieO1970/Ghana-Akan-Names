Description of the App

As a developer, one comes with different and new techniques of getting things and problems solved. So who said naming a child is easy? Most people are named differently. For example, one can be named with seasons, their favourite relatives, names from the bible,leaders,musicians,actors and so on. We go to Ghana where names are given according to the day of the week that one is born. From this we decided to come up with an app that will help identify the name one is given in Ghana depending on the day. This App helps the user to be able to get to know their names by the day they were born. Its easy to use and understanding it has been made easy as long as one remembers their date of birth, month and year.
